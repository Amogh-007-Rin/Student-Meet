student meet is a social platform where students from different academic niches can pair up for a study out. 
students with either similar or general acedemic background can invite other students around the city for a study out plan and go to near libraries and study places to study and have academic fun. 
the platform will also have a feature of live study rooms, related to same academic background and same level of studies, where students with similar subjects can join and study.

Example: 

1. Students with subject science can join a room for Science.
2. Students with subject mathematics can join a room for Mathematics.
3. Students with subject history can join a room for history.
-> Students in a room cannot speak the audio will be desabled by default and does not contains the feature of the audio.
-> Only video can be enabled and student can sit infront of the laptops and study, this helps the student feel not left alone and find a community of people who wants to be academically strong.
